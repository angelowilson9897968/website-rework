Questions to Assess Your Personal Risk Profile.

Do you prefer stable and predictable investments over high-return but risky ones?

Would you invest in a stock that has doubled in value in the past year but is highly volatile?

How would you react if your investment dropped 20% in a month?

Do you prefer a guaranteed 5% return or a potential 15% return with some risk?

If you had to choose, would you invest in stocks, bonds, or crypto?

Would you take a loan to invest if you were confident in high returns?

Do you prefer holding cash over investing in uncertain times?

How long is your ideal investment horizon?

How comfortable are you with investing in emerging markets?

Would you invest in a startup with high growth potential but also high risk?
